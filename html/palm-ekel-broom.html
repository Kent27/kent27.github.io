Palm Ekel Broom

Palm Ekel Broom is made of coconut leaf sticks and is especially chosen for its eco-friendly material and durability (not easily damaged). At Coco Produce, we sun dry Palm Ekel leaf sticks and tie them together in bulk. The sticks are obtained by combing the leaves contained in the bone leaves until it is completely cleaned. It is also relatively easy to be made into ready-to-use broom by tying the sticks together with a ribbon or a strong piece of string.
