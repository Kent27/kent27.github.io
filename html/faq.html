Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Coco Produce based in?

Our Plantation and Factory are distributed across three islands in Indonesia (Sumatra, Sulawesi, Java) with Headquarter in the capital city of Indonesia, Jakarta.

Are you a manufacturer or a trader?

We are a manufacturer with an established and registered company in Indonesia.

What are your products and production capacity?

1. Charcoal Briquette: ≥ 200 Metric Ton per month

2. Coconut Fiber: ≥ 180 Metric Ton per month

3. Coco Peat: ≥ 200 Metric Ton per month

4. Palm Ekel Broom: ≥ 200 Metric Ton per month

What is your packaging details and do you accept logo customization?

We accept logo and design customization

1. Charcoal Briquette: Master Box (Brown carton box) 10kg/carton and Inner box included upon request

2. Coconut Fiber: Pressed in bale: 90-120 kg/bale, then strapped by plastic straps

3. Coco Peat: Palletized, Blocks arranged on the wooden pallet and covered by plastic wrap/shrink film

4. Palm Ekel Broom: Gunny Sack or PP Bag 2kgs/Bundle, 50kg/Bag

You could also request the packaging details according to your standard

What payment and delivery terms do you accept/provide?

- Payment Terms: We accept Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), Letter of credit (L/C), Cash

- Delivery Terms: FOB, CNF, CIF, EXW

How do we request for sample products?

We provide free sample products and will be delivered through DHL, so that you can make sure of the product quality. Shipping cost is charged to customer for sample products.
