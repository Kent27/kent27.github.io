Our Honey

We supply Acacia Crassicarpa Honey with Apis Mellifera bee, which ranks top 5th in the world and 1st in Asia. Our honey characteristics are having slightly acidic taste, low in sucrose (< 5%), ideal HMF and Enzyme Diastase Activity, which is lab-ceritified from Sucofindo (the largest surveyor/assurance company in Indonesia).

Specification Description
Moisture < 21%
Sucrose < 5%
HMF ± 5 mg/kg
Enzyme Diastase Activity ± 6 DN
Water Insoluble Matter Nil

Source of water

Bee Colony and Beehive

QC Team and Packaging

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Our Beekeeping location based in?

Our Acacia Plantation and Beekeeping location is located in Palembang and Jambi city, the South Sumatra of Indonesia.

What are your products and production capacity?
