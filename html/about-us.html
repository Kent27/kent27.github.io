A few words about us

PT. Cocoproduce Ekspor Indonesia is Indonesia's Leading Coconut Products Supplier, Manufacturer and Exporter. Together with more than 200 local farmers, 1800 hectares of coconut plantation land and our highly experienced Quality Control and Logistics Staff, we are providing coconut products supply to more than 10 countries across 3 continents.

Our range of products consists of Coconut Shell Charcoal Briquette, Coco Fiber / Coir Fiber, Coco Peat, Semi Husked Coconut, and Palm Ekel Broom

Providing Authentic Indonesia's Coconut Quality

With Indonesia having the predicate of being the largest coconut plantation in the world, we ensure that every line of items produced by us are authentic and of the top quality.

From cultivation, harvest, manufacturing, storage to container stuffing, we are maintaining every single step of them with our Standard of Procedure done by experienced and professional staffs.

Become a Global Leader of Coconut Producer

Pioneering to utilize value-adding activities with better technolgy, upgraded infrastructure, maintaning networks of distribution and relations with local farmers, Cocoproduce has been able to deliver range of products that exceed our customer's expectations. We thereby earn their loyalty.
