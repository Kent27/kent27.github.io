Coco Peat

Coco Peat is natural fiber made out of coconut husks, which can be used as a 100% natural growing medium. At Cocoproduce, we manufacture Coco Peat in the form of Coco Peat Bricks, which are dehydrated and compressed blocks of coconut peat. Our drying procedure is using natural sun and free of chemical use, ensuring that our coco peat has great oxygenation properties and pH with neutral to slightly acidic, so that it also would be great to be mixed with alkaline garden soil.
