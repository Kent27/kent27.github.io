High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS)

High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) is a General Purpose Polystyrene that is combined with rubber (polybutadiene) to increase its durability and impact strength. We offer various kinds of PP to match your requirement.

Here is the specification of our HIPS.

Specification Description
Density 1.062 g/cm³
Tensile Yield 30.6 MPa
Tensile Modulus 2,300 MPa
Tensile Strength at Break 25.4 MPa
Elongation at Break 17.0 %
Flexural Strength 43.5 MPa
Flexural Modulus 1,760 MPa
Izod Impact 5-6 Kg/cm
Color Dark Grey and Black
