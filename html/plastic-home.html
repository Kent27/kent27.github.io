A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

PT. Plasticproduce Ekspor Indonesia is a manufacturer and exporter of plastic raw materials. We started off as a supplier to local injection factories. As local clients put more trust in us, we eventually received offer from overseas businesses and has since expanded our market to other countries. Our production site is currently located in Jakarta.

We are focused on results

Natural Material

Our products are manufactured using natural resources to ensure a superior quality of authentic products

Consistent Quality

We take quality control seriously in order to accurately deliver our products according to customers expectations.

Certified

We prepare certifications to ensure safety and a smooth exporting process.

Qualified Team

We maintain partnership with trusted suppliers and entrust our manufacturing process to qualified professionals.
