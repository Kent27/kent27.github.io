A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Polynate Plastics is a manufacturer of plastic raw materials. We started off as a supplier to local injection factories. As local clients put more trust in us, we eventually received offer from overseas businesses and has since expanded our market to other countries. Our production site is currently located in Jakarta.

We are focused on results

Natural Material

Our products are manufactured using natural resources to ensure a superior quality of authentic products

Consistent Quality

We take quality control seriously in order to accurately deliver our products according to customers expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Polynate Plastics based in?

Both our factory and headquarter is in the capital city of Indonesia, Jakarta.

Are you a manufacturer or a trader?

We are a manufacturer with an established and registered company in Indonesia.

What are your products and production capacity?

≥ 250 metric ton / month for each PP and HIPS, and 50 metric ton / month for PC Regrind

What payment and delivery terms do you accept/provide?

- Payment Terms: We accept Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), Letter of credit (L/C), Cash

- Delivery Terms: FOB, CNF, CIF, EXW

How do we request for sample products?

We provide free sample products and will be delivered through DHL/FedEx/EMS, so that you can make sure of the product quality. Shipping cost is charged to customer for sample products.
